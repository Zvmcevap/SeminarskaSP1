How to play:

  • Start the game by pressing the big round PLAY button bellow.
  • Use the movement buttons that show up after or
  • Q V P M keyboard keys to move RIGHT UP DOWN and LEFT respectfully. I ain't joking here lol
  • AUDIO QUEUES will inform you which direction to move into!
  • The EAR BUTTON in the center will repeat the direction tip. But you have ONLY 3!
  • Solve the three RIDDLES when prompted, to progress
  • But riddles come with REWARDS! Choose wisely!
  • Get to the end to recieve your SPECIAL PRIZE!!!
  • Should you forget click on the TREASURE CHEST above to open and close this menu
  • And most importantly, remember to HAVE FUN!
  • No wait... most important thing to remember is that only pain is gain... You'll figure it out soon enough...
